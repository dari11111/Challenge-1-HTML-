Our favorite places in Berlin

This site will show you well-known and also more hidden places in Berlin to visit.

Drachenberg

The Drachenberg gives you a wonderful view of Berlin and the Grundwald. You will have to climb a lot of stairs or a steep hill to climb for the view.

Ways to reach the top of Drachenberg

  • “The stairs of death” listed in google maps: Link zu Google Maps
  • From the Parkplatz at Teufelsseestraße
  • Up the Hill from S Heerstraße
